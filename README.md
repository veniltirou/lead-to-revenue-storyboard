## 📖 About This Project

This is an independently created educational storyboard series that demonstrates how **Oracle CX products work together end-to-end** — from the very first marketing campaign all the way through to subscription renewal.

It is designed for:
- Beginners exploring the Oracle CX ecosystem
- Functional consultants wanting to see cross-product integration
- Hiring managers and recruiters evaluating Oracle CX expertise

---

## 🏢 The Business Scenario

**ABC Corporation** is a specialty vending solutions provider with Oracle CX fully implemented across their business. They use Oracle CX products to manage their entire sales, service, order, billing and renewal operations.

**XYZ Corporation** is a networking company looking to equip their offices with vending solutions.

This storyboard follows ABC Corporation as they identify, pursue, close, fulfill and renew the deal with XYZ Corporation — using Oracle CX every step of the way.

## 🧩 Oracle CX Products Covered

| Product | Role in the Journey |
|---|---|
| Oracle CX Sales | Lead capture, qualification and opportunity management |
| Oracle CPQ | Configure, price, quote and approval |
| Oracle Order Management | Order orchestration and sequencing |
| Oracle Field Service | Field technician dispatch and installation |
| Oracle Subscription Management | Subscription activation and management |
| Oracle Fusion Service | Post-sale service and entitlement management |
| Oracle Accounts Receivable | Billing schedule and invoicing |
| Oracle Revenue Management (RMCS) | Revenue recognition and reporting |
| Renewal Management | Renewal preparation, upsell and cross-sell |
