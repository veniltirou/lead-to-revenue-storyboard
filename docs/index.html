Oracle CX Storyboard Series

A visual, beginner-friendly guide to understanding how Oracle CX products work together in a real B2B enterprise deal — from the very first marketing campaign all the way through to subscription renewal.

🏢 The Business Scenario

ABC Corporation is a specialty vending solutions provider with Oracle CX fully implemented across their business. They use Oracle CX to manage their entire sales, service, order, billing and renewal operations.

XYZ Corporation is a networking company that needs vending solutions for their offices. This series follows the complete journey as ABC Corporation identifies, pursues, closes, fulfills and renews the deal with XYZ Corporation — using Oracle CX every step of the way.

Oracle CX Products Covered in This Series

Oracle CX Sales Oracle CPQ Oracle Order Management Oracle Field Service Oracle Subscription Management Oracle Fusion Service Oracle Accounts Receivable Oracle Revenue Management Oracle Incentive Compensation Renewal Management
